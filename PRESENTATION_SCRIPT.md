# Fantasy AI ERP - Board Presentation Script

**Time Estimate:** 5 Minutes
**Setup:**
1. Open your terminal.
2. Run `uvicorn app:app --reload`.
3. Open `http://127.0.0.1:8000` in your web browser.
4. Share the browser window on Zoom.

---

## 1. Introduction (1 Minute)
"Good afternoon. I am presenting the **Fantasy AI MVP**, an intelligent system designed to automate our core operations at **Fantasy Bakers Ltd**."

"We identified three bottlenecks: Procurement delays, Social Media consistency, and Delivery freshness. This MVP addresses all three using localized AI."

## 2. Live Demo (3 Minutes)

**(Click on the 'Smart Inventory' Card)**
"First, **Procurement**. The system scans our rolling sales data. As you can see, it automatically flagged 'Sugar' as low stock and suggested a 50kg reorder. This removes human error and ensures we never run out of ingredients."

**(Click on the 'AI Social Pipeline' Card)**
"Second, **Marketing**. Our staff just uploads a video. Watch this."
*(Type: 'Hot fresh bread coming out of the oven')*
*(Click 'Generate Caption')*
"The AI instantly creates captions in both English and, crucially, **Coastal Swahili** slang ('Vibe la Pwani'), while tagging our official handle `@fantasybakersheavenlytreats`. This ensures brand consistency."

**(Click on the 'Tuktuk Logistics' Card)**
"Third, **Logistics**. We use a Freshness Algorithm."
*(Click 'Dispatch AI')*
"It calculated the optimal route to 'Cafe Mocha' to ensure the bread arrives at **above 50°C**. It assigned Driver 'Juma' because his route score was highest."

**(Click on 'Simulate Breach' in Security Card)**
"Finally, **Compliance**. We are fully ODPC compliant. If I simulate a security breach... you see it is instantly logged in our audit trail for KEBS."

## 3. Q&A / Next Steps (1 Minute)
"This system is built on **Supabase** and **Python**, meaning it can scale from 1 bakery to 100 with no code changes. We are ready for pilot deployment."
